About Us

Skillatinaya Workshops bridges the crucial gap between academic knowledge and industry demands. We are driven by a mission to empower students and professionals with job-ready, practical skills in cutting-edge technologies like AI, Data Science, and Python.

Our sessions are led by currently working professional mentors who bring real-world case studies and project methodologies directly into the classroom. We focus on true hands-on experience, ensuring every participant leaves with a tangible portfolio and the confidence to apply their learning immediately.
